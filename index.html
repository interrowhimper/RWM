ᏣᎳᎩ ᎦᏬᏂᎯᏍᏗ Root Word Method Drill

Welcome to the Root Word Method drill! This drill is intended to

help you work on conjugating verbs and, optionally, adding affixes.

You're responsible for checking your own work! Please rely on materials

generated by first-language speakers of Cherokee to build your understanding

of verbs. As you're working, don't forget your root suffixes, and be sure

to consider whether all the affixes and tenses are compatible with one another.
